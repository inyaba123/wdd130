university of nigeria (unn)

The University of Nigeria, Enugu Campus (UNEC) is one of the three main campuses of the University of Nigeria, located in Ogui, Enugu State. It was developed from the former Nigerian College of Arts, Science and Technology and became part of the University of Nigeria system after its establishment. UNEC serves as a key academic and administrative hub, hosting several faculties such as Law, Business Administration, Environmental Studies, and Health Sciences.
